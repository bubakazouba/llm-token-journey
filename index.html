The Journey of a Token

How a large language model actually works — followed from the point of view of a single word as it flows through the network. Each component has its own sub-tabs; click into anything.

Running example: "The cat sat on the ___"  →  we ride the token cat until the model predicts the blank.

Numbers here are toy-sized (4-dimensional vectors, tiny vocabulary) so you can watch them change. A real model uses thousands of dimensions and billions of parameters — but the mechanism on screen is the real one, just shrunk.
